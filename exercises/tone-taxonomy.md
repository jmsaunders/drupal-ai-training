1. Create a Tone vocabulary.
2. Add terms (e.g., Happy, Sad, Angry, Mine, David Attenborough).
3. Write descriptive guidance in each term description.
4. In your text format, enable AI → Tone and point it at the Tone vocabulary.
5. Test in CKEditor by selecting text and applying a tone change.
