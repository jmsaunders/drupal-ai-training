## Why this matters
AI is reshaping content workflows. Drupal’s approach emphasises human-in-the-loop design, provider choice, and transparent governance.

## Drupal AI initiative (high level)
- Goal: unify the many AI modules under a clear product vision and responsible practices.
- Focus: augment editors and builders; avoid lock-in through multiple providers.
- Outputs to watch: AI Core, AI Agents, recipes, search, editorial assistants.

## What participants will learn
- Where AI fits in Drupal’s authoring and ops layers
- The building blocks: Core AI module, providers, prompts, agents, and recipes
- Real examples you can reproduce on a clean site

> Tip: keep “people over prompts.” Design the workflow, then select models and recipes.
