## Core principles
- Transparency: how decisions are made and logged
- Privacy: user control of data flows and storage
- Fairness: detect and reduce bias

## Accessibility
- Apply WCAG principles (Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust).
- Include disabled users in testing.
- Keep human support paths and explainability for critical interactions.

## Operational guidance
- Prefer provider options that support data boundaries and regional processing.
- Monitor outputs for bias; iterate prompts and recipes when issues are found.
